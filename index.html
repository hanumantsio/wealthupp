Why Daily SIP?

Daily SIP Beats Monthly SIP Every Time

Daily SIP investing offers superior rupee cost averaging compared to monthly SIPs. By investing every day instead of once a month, you spread your investments across 30 times more market price points, significantly reducing the impact of market volatility on your portfolio.

✓

Better Rupee Cost Averaging

Invest at 30+ price points per month instead of just one, smoothing out market fluctuations and reducing average purchase cost over time.

✓

No Market Timing Stress

Daily investing eliminates the anxiety of choosing the right day to invest. Your money flows in consistently regardless of market highs or lows.

✓

Power of Compounding

More frequent investments mean your returns start compounding earlier. Over 10+ years, daily SIPs can generate significantly higher corpus than monthly SIPs of the same total amount.

✓

Disciplined Wealth Building

Daily auto-debits build an unbreakable investment habit. Small daily amounts feel effortless while delivering powerful long-term results through consistency and discipline.
